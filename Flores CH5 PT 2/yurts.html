The Yurts at Pacific Trails Resort

What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permamnet structures four feet off the ground. sEach yurt is fully enclosed with canvas wals, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened
How are the yurts furnished?
Esch yurt is furnished with a queen sized bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guests packcomfortable walking shoes and plan todress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
